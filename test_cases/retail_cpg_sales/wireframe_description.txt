RETAIL/CPG SALES DASHBOARD WIREFRAME DESCRIPTION
================================================

Dashboard Title: Sales Performance Overview
Time Period Selector: [Daily | Weekly | Monthly | Quarterly | YTD] with date range picker

LAYOUT: 4 Rows x 3 Columns Grid

ROW 1 - KEY METRICS CARDS (Full Width)
---------------------------------------
[Card 1: Total Revenue]
- Large number display: $12.5M
- Trend indicator: ↑ +12.3% vs Last Period
- Sparkline showing last 7 periods
- Green background if positive, red if negative

[Card 2: Gross Profit]
- Large number display: $4.4M
- Profit Margin %: 35.2%
- Trend indicator: ↑ +8.7% vs Last Period
- Mini bar chart showing margin trend

[Card 3: Tons Moved]
- Large number display: 2,847 MT
- Trend indicator: ↑ +5.2% vs Last Period
- Efficiency metric: $4,395/ton
- Volume gauge chart (0-5000 scale)

ROW 2 - TREND ANALYSIS (Full Width)
------------------------------------
[Combined Line Chart]
- Title: Revenue, Profit & Volume Trends
- X-axis: Time periods (last 12 months)
- Y-axis 1 (left): Revenue & Profit ($)
- Y-axis 2 (right): Tons Moved
- Three lines: Revenue (blue), Profit (green), Tons (orange)
- Interactive tooltip showing all values on hover
- Option to toggle between absolute and % change view

ROW 3 - PERFORMANCE BREAKDOWN (3 Columns)
-----------------------------------------
[Column 1: Revenue by Category]
- Donut chart with legend
- Categories: Beverages (45%), Snacks (30%), Dairy (25%)
- Click to drill down to subcategories
- Show $ and % for each segment

[Column 2: Top 10 Products]
- Horizontal bar chart
- Product names on Y-axis
- Revenue on X-axis
- Color coding by profit margin (gradient)
- Show exact values on hover

[Column 3: Regional Performance]
- Heat map or filled map
- Regions colored by revenue intensity
- Click region for detailed metrics
- Legend showing revenue ranges

ROW 4 - DETAILED ANALYTICS (3 Columns)
---------------------------------------
[Column 1: Customer Segment Analysis]
- Stacked bar chart
- Segments: Large Chains, Independent, Online
- Show revenue and profit for each
- Period-over-period comparison

[Column 2: Store Type Performance]
- Bubble chart
- X-axis: Number of stores
- Y-axis: Average revenue per store
- Bubble size: Total tons moved
- Color by store type

[Column 3: Operational Metrics Table]
- Inventory Turnover: 12.5x
- Avg Order Value: $487
- Active Customers: 1,234
- Products per Order: 18.7
- Fill Rate: 96.8%

INTERACTIVE FEATURES:
- Filter Panel (collapsible): Product Category, Region, Customer Type, Store Type, Brand
- Export buttons: PDF, Excel, PNG
- Refresh indicator with last update timestamp
- Drill-down capability on all charts
- Responsive design for tablet/mobile viewing

COLOR SCHEME:
- Primary: #0C62FB (Blue)
- Success: #2ECC71 (Green)
- Warning: #F39C12 (Orange)
- Danger: #E74C3C (Red)
- Neutral: #95A5A6 (Gray)
